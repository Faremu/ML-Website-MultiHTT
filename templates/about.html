About

1. About Multi-HTT

Multi-HTT is a machine learning-based web application designed to predict the yields of three major products from hydrothermal treatment (HTT) of biomass: Bio-oil, Bio-char, and Bio-gas. The predictive model was developed using the XGBoost algorithm trained on 1,010 data points collected from 180 published studies (2011â€“2025).

2. How It Works

Users can input 15 features including: Operating conditions: Residence time, Temperature, Pressure, Biomass loading, Reactor size, Water content, Water-to-BL ratio Elemental properties: C, H, N, O, S, H/C, O/C, HHV The model simultaneously predicts the yields of Bio-oil, Bio-char, and Bio-gas (in %w/w).

4. Disclaimer

This tool is intended for research and educational purposes only. Predictions are based on literature-derived data and may not reflect all real-world conditions. Users should validate results experimentally before practical applications.
